Romantic journey

Whether you are celebrating your honeymoon or just want to run away with your partner, we offer the most romantic vacation in picturesque Ukraine

Tunnel of love

The romantic tunnel, which is laid rail tracks is located near the town of Klevan.The tunnel appeared due to a natural phenomenon. The branches of trees and bushes intertwined with each other and formed a natural arch, which has a fairly regular geometric shapes. There is a very powerful energy, and two tracks seem to symbolize the fate that goes hand in hand. It is believed that when lovers visit here, they will be fine. And if someone comes alone and makes a wish to find his other half, it will definitely come true.The town of Klevan itself will also be interesting for a walk. It has preserved several ancient cathedrals and the remains of a fortress of the XV century.

Romantic city of Lviv

A charming, European, ancient corner of Ukraine. Lviv is a city of dreams and hobbies. And, probably, the most suitable for seeing such a large number of cafes, squares, fountains is probably not in any city in Ukraine.Lviv residents, as residents of the cultural capital of Ukraine, know a thing or two about beauty. And you, with your partner, can feel a little like a Lviv resident. Lvivians with Italian roots. Here is such a funny symbiosis. Visit the Italian courtyard in the immediate vicinity of the Market Square. The courtyard itself is surrounded by snow-white walls, balconies and columns. It was built with the romantic feelings of Jan Sobieski, King of Poland. The monarch loved to spend time with his lady of the heart. And still here lovers spend time together, admiring pictures, flower beds, enjoying fragrant Lviv coffee and, of course, each other.Local lovers and married couples share a secret - if you whisper to couples about their love and wish eternal happiness, then everything will surely come true.

Lake Lemuria

An unusual lake with bright pink water and shores of snow-white salt crystals is hidden away from human eyes, in the south of Kherson region. One of the most fabulous lakes in Ukraine is located near the village of Hryhorivka, 100 kilometers from Kherson. On the west coast of the Sea of Azov stretches a whole system of shallow salt bays - Sivash. According to legend, this is where the Lemurian Sea once stretched, which washed the shores of the mythical sunken continent with a highly developed civilization. 35%, while in the Dead Sea - only 27-30%. The water here is so salty that it is impossible to drown. You can sunbathe without leaving the water - salt will keep your body afloat at all times. Due to the high concentration of salt, as well as the extremely rich chemical composition of the water in the lake has healing properties.

Odessa is beautiful at any time of the year - even after the end of the holiday season, hundreds of tourists come here. The air in this seaside city is imbued with romance, which can be felt just walking through the famous courtyards of Odessa. Together with your other half, be sure to visit the beach "Langeron" in Odessa, try ice cream on Primorsky Boulevard from numerous establishments on Deribasovskaya. When was the last time you confessed your love to your other half? Maybe you are looking for the right moment? In Odessa, many reasons, and here's another - the descent of Kangun and the Ladder of Lovers, which are located just behind the monument to De Ribas. Stepping on every step, you need to say to each other words of love and other pleasures - and you will hear them throughout life. Do not believe - check for yourself.

Khablovsky lighthouse

In the south of the country there are original places for lovers. On the border of Kherson and Mykolayiv regions there is an amazing location - a lighthouse in the steppe. This is one of the three Hubble lighthouses. Here you can hide from the world and enjoy nature. This is a beautiful and unusual location for declaration. Surprise your loved one: organize a photo shoot against the backdrop of the tower and the endless steppe. Unforgettable impressions are guaranteed! Or arrange a romantic picnic, the atmosphere is conducive. And yet, you will be surprised, but the tower can "sing": in windy weather you can hear low and long sounds, similar to the throat singing of Tuvans.

Island of Love in Ternopil

Ternopil is a city-park, so in the autumn it is especially beautiful here. Among the sights of the city are the Ternopil pond, the castle palace, the water park "Topilche" and, of course, the islands of Love and Separation. These two unique islands are located in the middle of Ternopil lakes. The island of love has long been a place for meetings and meetings of couples in love. From the park to the island of Love can be reached via a small bridge. In the middle of the island is a gazebo surrounded by willows. Under the branches of willows are romantic benches.

Vinnytsia is romantic

Do you know that in Vinnytsia there are many interesting, cozy and lovely places for romantic walks ?! Some places, well, are soooo unexpected. After the opening of the Roshen fountain and the reconstruction of the embankment, this place has become almost the most favorite for every Vinnytsia resident. When the fountain is open, in the spring and summer season, the place is too crowded. But what could be better than watching the light and music show of the Roshen Fountain and then walking along the waterfront in the night city lights. It will be especially beautiful at dusk, when the lights of the night city are reflected in the water.
