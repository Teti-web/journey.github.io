Find The Best Place

Always want to visit interesting and unusual places, enjoying the color and originality of foreign landscapes. On the territory of Ukraine there are many places, visiting which, experienced travelers do not immediately mention that are not in Africa, Italy, the Netherlands, Germany or other no less interesting country. We have collected a selection of such places in today's article. So, let's go on our trip around the world!

Attractions

Every corner of Ukraine is unique. The West is picturesque and cosy, the North and East are industrial and monumental, while the South is always charming and welcoming. Each region contains its own abundance of beautiful and historic tourists sites.

Accomodation

Ukraine is friendly to backpackers and you easily get lifts elsewhere in the country

Food

The peculiar features of traditional Ukrainian food have been forming over centuries. The neighboring countries, climate conditions, rich soil and hard-working locals have influenced the complexity of the dishes. Ukrainian dishes are generously flavored (with garlic often the main seasoning) and, despite the contradictory tastes being used, come together in a harmonious blend.

Kamenets-Podolsky

A city that can be told a lot, and the richness of its history can only be envied. But it is better to see once than to hear the wonderful cries of acquaintances. Kamenets-Podolsky is a vivid example of a medieval town, where there is an atmosphere of impregnability, strength and at the same time boundless romance. Every year, many festivals are held in large areas of the city, such as "Respublika". People from all over the country come to the street art festival, listen to their favorite artists, make new acquaintances and trample on shoes.

.Address: Khmelnytsky region, Kamyanets-Podilsky city

Ukrainian Dead Sea

Ukraine has its own Dead Sea, located near Kherson. There it is called Lemurian, because the water in this lake is colored pink. But the miracles do not end there, because this water is considered healing and rejuvenating. The most interesting thing is that in our Lemurian lake 5% more salt than in the Dead Sea!

Address: Kherson region, Grigorivka village

Skalat Castle

There are several theories surrounding its appearance. According to one of them, the residents of the city suffered from government harassment and constantly held protests. In response to government pressure, the rebellious people even burned down the city hall. The nobility was very concerned about the mood of the locals, so began construction of the fortress.Another version states that the castle was a dowry of Krzysztofor Wichrowski's daughter and passed into the possession of Jan Firlejow, a castellan.Skalat Castle is located by the river in the southwestern part of Skalat.

Address: Ternopil region, Skalat city

Rafting on the Prut River

Prut is another of the most famous, extreme and interesting rivers in Ukraine. It is popular among rafters for the nature of the current, difficult rapids and a later season for rafting. The main rapids and passes are located in the area from Vorokhta to Yaremche. Rafting on the Prut River is held in the low water season, when not only experienced athletes but also beginners will be able to try their hand.

Address: Ukrainian Carpathians

"Round yard"

Initially, the "Round Yard" was planned as a defensive structure for protection against the Tatars. Large in size (over 60 meters in length and 40 meters in width), it is a small fortress with deaf high walls and four round towers around the perimeter. Now the "Round Yard" has been restored, various cultural events are held here. It is adjoined by a beautiful park with Golitsyn Palace (19th century), an arboretum, lakes and wooden sculptures.

Address: Sumy region, the city of Trostyanets

Kobleve

The hospitable coast of the Nikolaev area will please you with blue water, sandy beaches and interesting entertainments.

.Address: Odessa region, Koblevo city

Sofiyivka Park

The famous park "Sofiyivka" in Uman - a masterpiece of garden and park art of world level, a sample of landscape composition of water, earth, architectural structures and sculptures.

Address: Cherkasy region, Uman city

Lightning Festival

Malanka in Bukovyna is an original theatrical carnival in which men and young people take part. Locals write original scenarios for the holiday, create their own costumes and masks. And already on January 14 this action can be seen on city streets.Thanks to it's unique, crazy atmosphere of fun, the festival attracts thousands of visitors from Bukovina and all over Ukraine. Malanka Festival is an ideal opportunity to spend your winter vacation fun, unforgettable in the style of ancient Ukrainian traditions.

Address: Chernivtsi region
