Extreme travel

Life is an adventure, not a tour package! Travel to not just watch. Choose extreme tours to try, listen and feel! These phrases become especially important when only you and your backpack are far from home. Live this moment, enjoy freedom and do things that seem completely meaningless in "real life"!

Rafting

Rafting is very popular today in the western world. This extreme tour is not for the faint of heart. Rafting on fast rivers in inflatable latex boats is practiced, the crew is usually 3-10 people. A person here requires maximum physical endurance, willpower, and in return you enjoy the amazing nature and get incomparable feelings. Rafting on a mountain river on rafts is a fun adventure and a test at the same time. Speed and speed of reaction! Waves and rapids! Rapid current and squeak of fun! Ukraine has excellent natural conditions for the development of rafting, and over the years interest in it is growing rapidly. There are 5 most popular regions: the Carpathians (rivers Cherny Cheremosh, Prut, White Cheremosh, Black and White Tisza); Dniester (plain part); The lower reaches of the Southern Bug River; Kyiv region (rivers Desna, Ros); Zhytomyr region (Teterev river).

Diving

This is extreme tourism for those who want to get acquainted with the extraordinary underwater world. You need to try it at least once, and then you can say with confidence that you have seen a lot in this life. Nowadays, there are many places for this type of extreme tourism. If you wish, you can simply admire the beauty of coral reefs and sea creatures, or explore sunken ships and the remains of ancient cities. The most popular places for diving in Ukraine: Odessa Underwater Museum, Malinsky Quarry, Pashinsky Quarry, Sokolovsky Quarry.

Speleo

Speleo is a special kind of extreme tourism. Extreme caves are something special: underground rivers, lakes, waterfalls, huge halls… These are kilometers of caves with only emptiness and silence. Do not deny yourself the pleasure of listening to this silence. The most popular caves in Ukraine: Zvirynetsky Caves and Archangel Michael Monastery (Kiev), Ilyinsky Monastery (Anthony's Caves) (Chernihiv), Bakota Rock and Cave Monastery (Khmelnytsky region), Kryshtaleva Cave (Kryvcheny), Far Caves (Kryvche), Far Caves monastery in Rozgirche.

Mountaineering

Mountaineering is considered to be the most extreme vacation. Today, mountaineering is a whole industry that is evenly developing and popularizing. As a rule, it is customary to choose summer for vacations, when the weather allows to reach the planned peak with minimal losses. However, fans of thrills do not stop in winter. And difficult weather conditions and the avalanche only add to the density of travel. They go to the mountains to test themselves, to take risks, to overcome everything and to reach the top. To do this, you need to be well physically prepared. The most popular places for mountaineering in Ukraine: Deneshi rock district on the banks of the Teterev River 20 km from Zhytomyr, an abandoned granite quarry in Korostyshiv, fabulous rocks in Buki, picturesque rocks of Yuzhnoukrainsk.

Rope jumping

Rope jumping is jumping from relatively low objects (bridges, buildings, towers, pipes and steep cliffs) using climbing ropes. Rope jumping is carried out all over Ukraine, but the most extreme is this type of recreation in Kamianets-Podilskyi on the highest bridge of Ukraine - "Swift Doe". In rope jumping (rope jumping) only special climbing equipment is used, which requires regular and thorough inspection, constant monitoring and appropriate operating conditions.
